Lady Finger Cookies

Yields 50-60 cookies

---
Frosting:  
1/4 cup softened butter
2 cups sifted powdered sugar
2 Tbsp whole milk or cream
1/2 tsp almond extract
2 drops of red food coloring

Cream butter and sugar. Add extract and red food coloring and mix until frosting is light pink.
---

---
Cookies:
2 1/4 cup flour (sifted)
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/4 cup softened butter
3/4 cup sifted powdered sugar
1 tsp almond extract
1 1/4 cup rolled oats (old-fashioned oats)
3/4 cup minced almonds

Preheat over to 325 degrees.
Cream butter and sugar gradually until fluffy. 
Mix in the dry ingredients: sifted flour, salt, and then the rolled oats.
Using about 2 tsp of dough for each cookie, roll into a ball and then lengthen into an oblong about 2 1/2".
Bake on an ungreased baking sheet for 20 minutes or until the ends just start to brown.
Let the cookies fully cool on a wire rack. 
Spread icing on the cookies and sprinkle with almonds before icing dries.
