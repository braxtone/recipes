Lady Finger Cookies --

Cookies:
2 1/4 cup flour (sifted)
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/4 cup butter
3/4 cup sifted confectioner's sugar
1 Tsp. Almond extract
1-1/4 cup rolled oats 

Mix and sift flour and salt. Cream butter add sugar gradually and cream until fluffy. Add dry ingredients and mix add rolled oats and mix. Using about 1 tsp of dough for each cookie shape into oblong about 2 1/2" long. Bake on ungreased baking sheet in preheated 325 degree oven about 20 min. Cool, dip one end of each cookie in frosting and sprinkle with almounds. 

6 doz  

Frosting:  
1/4 C butter
2 C sifted confectioner's sugar
2 T cream (I use millk)
1/2 tsp almond extract
1 drop of red food color

Combine butter sugar, cream and mix well. Add extract and red food coloring and mix. Put 3/4 cup of minced almonds for icing
